Chapter 101: Ambition

Seeing Baron Kate leaving indignantly, Hudson originally wanted to give him the wild geese he had hunted to comfort his wounded heart.

But thinking of the meaning of the number thirteen in the continent of Aslant, I finally gave up the idea of ​​seeking excitement.

You can't do good things with bad intentions. People have finally agreed to something after a lot of effort. If you continue to provoke them, they might really go crazy.

It was estimated that the hunting of geese just now was just a coincidence, but in the eyes of Baron Katelai, it was a deliberate "threat" created by him.

Heaven and earth swear to God, “Five arrows shot at thirteen geese, every arrow went through their heads”, is this something a normal person can shoot?

Even if Hudson was cheating, he still needed the cooperation of the geese. If the geese were not changing their formation and were caught by the arrow, there would be no such miracle.

However, Hudson's pretense was so successful that Baron Kate was completely confused and he didn't even think about it as a coincidence.

Only firing five arrows was not just for showing off; what was more important was that in order to achieve such a range, Hudson could only fire five arrows.

If there were more, his arm strength would be too much. Who made him just a weak rookie who had just touched the threshold of intermediate knight?

With ordinary bow and arrow shooting, one can easily fire more than a hundred arrows; with the high-strength elven bow, under normal circumstances, one cannot even fully draw the bow, so there are naturally limitations in its use.

Unless he lowers his shooting range and reduces his arm strength, his sustained output as a strong archer will be truly impressive.

Hudson's transport brigade had not yet been formed, but the Second Infantry Regiment that entered Arbor County had already completed two rounds of dragnet searches in conjunction with local nobles, and was just short of turning the land over.

After all the trouble, I spent more than half a month without seeing any trace of the Skull and Bones Society.

If it weren't for the evidence of Knight Florian and the bodies of fifty-seven soldiers, as well as the testimonies of a number of eyewitnesses, everyone would have doubted whether the news of the Skeleton Society's appearance was just a rumor.

As they couldn't find anyone for a long time, instead of relaxing, everyone became even more nervous.

The reason why cult organizations are so scary is that they hide in the dark. If their identities were exposed, they would be easier to deal with.

Unfortunately, almost a month has passed since the incident, and still nothing has been found.

"Perhaps the members of the Skull and Bones Society have already left Arbor County. These rats are best at hiding. According to past practice, they would flee thousands of miles away after each crime.

Although we were not able to capture the Skeleton Lord this time, it was not a complete loss for everyone.

We successfully destroyed the dens of several cult organizations, including the Cult of the Evil God, the Cult of Chaos, the Church of Desire, etc. We also destroyed three smuggling salt gangs and arrested a total of 236 criminals of various types.

I believe that after this battle, the social order in Qiaomu County will be…” Listening to Baron Raphael’s summary of the results of the battle, Viscount Oran’s face was terribly gloomy. Most of these criminals were caught in Qiaomu City, and they just said that he, the city lord, was not good at governing the place.

However, the facts were before him, and he could not find any reason to refute them. Baron Raphael was sent by the Governor's Office to help, so he did not need to buy into his claims.

"Baron Raphael is right. We have achieved a lot in this period. But these are just a bunch of small fry. They have nothing to do with our goal.

If we want to truly maintain long-term peace and stability, we must continue to work hard to wipe out the Skull and Bones Society in one fell swoop.

This evil cult has launched five rebellions since entering our Alpha Kingdom.

The previous few incidents are still far away from us and can be ignored for the time being, but the last rebellion in the counties of Wright and Whiteton was experienced by everyone personally.

I thought they would run away thousands of miles away immediately after their failure as usual, but now they appear again.

Does this mean that they have new plans in Qiaomu County or the Southeast Province?

In short, if we don’t remove this cancer, our Southeast Province will have trouble sleeping and eating!”

Viscount Oran said seriously.

It is impossible to stop the search operation. Who knows if the Skull and Bones Society will make a comeback after everyone disbands the team?

He would risk exposing his identity, it couldn't be just to kill Knight Florian, right?

A knight, where did this card come from?

The revenge for the murder of his father, the hatred for the abduction of his wife, or was it that Knight Florian dug up the ancestral grave of the Holy Leader of the Skull and Bones Society?

They don't look alike at all!

The deceased Knight Florian was not a very decent person, but at least he was not a vicious criminal, and he had a good reputation in the aristocratic circle.

"Viscount Oran, the Governor has ordered martial law throughout the province and strictly checked all major transportation routes and docks. If the Skull and Bones Society members show up, they will be discovered immediately.

Especially the roads in and out of Arbor County were the focus of monitoring. If the people of the Skull and Bones Society were smart enough, they would not show up at this time.

Perhaps they have entered the northern province of Furen or the western province of Fengyun. This is beyond our jurisdiction and can only be dealt with by the Governor's Office sending a letter to the two provinces."

Baron Raphael analyzed.

Unlike the local nobles of Qiaomu County who can stay here forever, the Second Infantry Regiment still has its own tasks.

It's ok to come and help occasionally, but what about the port if we stay in Qiaomu County for a long time?

After being transferred away for such a long time, wouldn’t it be embarrassing if the boss found out that the port could operate normally with or without them?

The slogan of preventing the invasion of sea tribes has been shouted for countless years, but unfortunately, the port where the Second Infantry Regiment is stationed has not seen any trace of sea tribes for hundreds of years.

As for the invasion of sea tribe, there has been no invasion of sea tribe since the birth of Alpha Kingdom.

The coastal defense line that was once heavily armed has long been abandoned, and the only remaining task is to fight pirates.

But that is also the task of the navy. These landlubbers usually just maintain order in the port.

In fact, there was nothing to maintain. The port where the Second Infantry Regiment was stationed was the fiefdom of the Dalton family, and they had the ability to govern it.

The reason they are being transferred here to handle the affairs of Qiaomu County now, besides because they are the closest, the most important thing is that they have the least time.

The nobles in the province had long called for the disbanding of the Second Infantry Regiment. After all, no one wanted to share the military expenses and support a useless army.

As for the kingdom's coastal defense forces, they had already been deployed to the northern frontier one after another. The task of defending against the sea tribes was directly delegated to the major provinces.

Without the enemy's coastal defense, there is naturally no defense. It is said that in the deep sea now, various ethnic groups are holding PK friendly matches, and the fight is more intense than on the mainland.

Without waiting for a result, the probability of getting ashore is slim.

After all, we are not species from the same dimension, and the marine species that come ashore can easily become a delicacy on the human table.

…

In Beda City, after receiving the message from his men, Earl Pierce spat out two words indifferently. "Good-for-nothing!"

Not being able to find any trace of the Skull and Bones Society is the second most important thing. At least we can capture a few important guys to make up the numbers!

After making such a big fuss, he ended up catching a bunch of scumbags. How could this governor end up well?

If it really doesn't work, catching the illegal salt dealers and digging deep to uncover the salt gangs behind the scenes is also acceptable.

Who doesn't know that the salt trade in the southeastern province is the Dalton family business?

Daring to sell illegal salt clearly shows that he does not take the "King of Southeast" seriously.

There is no doubt that the smooth operation of private salt trading must have been supported by local nobles, or at least tacitly approved.

There is no way. Who made the profit from salt so huge?

Tens of thousands of salt cooks along the coast generate huge profits for the Dalton family every year, and it is the monopoly trade that stabilizes the profits from salt.

To be precise, there was no such thing as private salt in the Kingdom of Alpha at first, and the kingdom's laws did not stipulate who could sell salt.

However, with the rise of the great nobles, they gradually monopolized the kingdom's salt trade. Everyone reached an agreement with the kingdom to pay a certain amount of financial resources every year in exchange for the salt monopoly of a certain place.

However, these are just unspoken rules and have not been put into legal documents. Basically, whoever has the bigger fist will make the deal.

Once a big family falls into decline, these interests will also be transferred. Whether or not you can keep these interests depends entirely on your own strength.

It's too late now. Those who dare to sell illegal salt may not be very strong, but they must have a good nose.

It is a basic operation to flee immediately when you sense danger. If you are a step slower, the nobles who originally supported them secretly will often be the first to raise their butcher knives to attack them.

"Tell the Second Infantry Regiment to continue hunting down the Skull and Bones Society. If they can't catch anyone, they shouldn't come back."

Earl Pierce said coldly.

It was not just a vent of anger, but more of a political need for him to make a gesture. The news that the Skull and Bones Society had appeared in Qiaomu County had spread, and a letter for help had already entered the Governor's Mansion.

If the Governor's Office did not take action, and there were subsequent changes in the local area, or a repeat of the events in the counties of Wright and Wyton, Earl Pierce would not be able to prevent it.

The kingdom could not tolerate an incompetent governor, and the nobles of the southeastern province could not tolerate an irresponsible governor.

Even though the Dalton family is powerful, it doesn't mean that the governor cannot be replaced. If it really comes to that, the first to request a change will most likely be the Dalton family.

If he takes the initiative, he can also take the position of successor governor. It is better to cook the meat in his own pot than to have the kingdom send someone over.

…

In the Mountain Territory, after more than twenty days of overtime work and by moving other undelivered orders from all over the place, Hudson finally got all the goods together.

The messy stuff together filled up forty carriages. In addition to weapons and equipment, there were also a lot of pots and pans.

On the contrary, we didn't bring much food with us. Apart from some necessary dry food, we had to eat the rest as we walked.

Hudson was very relieved in this regard. With his reputation as the "Knight of the Divine Bow", he would not have to worry about not being able to buy food on the road.

If you are a little thick-skinned and eat for free along the way, you can transport the goods.

Looking at Hudson's lineup, Knight Adrian was horrified and said:

"Hudson, you're only going to take a hundred soldiers with you?"

Everyone's faces were not looking very good. The reason why they dared to accept the order at this time was because they relied on the elite troops in Hudson's hands.

It has to be said that Hudson was quite cunning. The knights he invited to escort him were not only poor, but also did not have any comrades from the Fifth Army.

Everyone only heard about his brilliant achievements, and had no idea how much truth there was in them. As a result, everyone's confidence in him was a hundred times stronger than his own.

"Don't we have you guys? Just forty truckloads of goods, four hundred men are more than enough."

Hudson said calmly.

If it was just for transporting goods, this configuration would definitely be high-end. On average, ten people would have one car, and it could be pushed all the way to Arbor County.

But now that the traces of the Skull and Bones Society have been discovered, everyone's confidence is not so strong. Especially since it is said that the one who killed Knight Florian was a golden knight, which makes everyone even more uncertain.

There are high-level knights, evil god priests, and various evil summoning techniques. Once the fight starts, you can imagine how tragic the scene will be.

"Respected Baron Hudson, I beg you not to joke around like this and take your main force with you!"

Adrian said unhappily.

Deep down in his heart, he couldn't help but complain secretly: We are all family, don't betray your brothers like this. If something unexpected happens, it will be a tragedy.

Giving everyone a reassuring look, Hudson smiled calmly and said, "You worry too much! Things are not as bad as you think. As long as our luck is not too bad, we should be able to go smoothly.

The Skull and Bones Society did show up in Arbor County, but that was a month ago. The Governor's Office has sent an infantry regiment to assist the local nobles and arrest the remnants of the Skull and Bones Society.

No matter how inefficient they were, they had cleaned up Arbor County. It was unknown whether those rats who had been hiding in the dark corners were still alive.

The nobles along the way also stepped up their vigilance, and set up checkpoints on all major traffic routes, waiting for the Skull and Bones Society to arrive.

We are leading the convoy forward. We will not take shortcuts or take small roads. We are all family members along the way. What is there to be afraid of?

Even if the Skeleton Lord really came, he would have avoided us. Could it be that the remaining three or five of their followers could rob our convoy?"

It was easy to say, but in fact Hudson was betting that the Skull and Bones Society would look down on his team.

Although weapons and equipment are good, they need people to use them. The most important thing for the Skull and Bones Society now is hiding. Even if they robbed the convoy, they couldn't go on the road with such a big target.

Acting rashly would only increase the chances of them being exposed and attract more hunting forces.

The crowd, who could not find sufficient reasons to refute, were reluctantly persuaded by Hudson. Now that they were here, they couldn't just go home because they were afraid of the risks, right?

This kind of thing is not what a knight should do.

What's more, everyone needs the money, so there is no reason to withdraw.

Seeing that people's hearts were barely willing to cooperate, Hudson was too lazy to continue talking nonsense and simply waved his hand to announce his departure.

Giving timely assistance is much more valuable than adding icing on the cake.

While the nobles of Qiaomu County have not yet recovered from their fear of the Skull and Bones Society, sending the goods over first can earn you a lot of favors.

When the time comes and the risk has been completely passed, it is just a business.

…

The convoy moved forward slowly, and the biting cold wind hit everyone's faces, telling of the arrival of winter.

Winter in the south is a magical attack, and traveling in this season is really not a good choice.

Fortunately, the people accompanying them were all elite soldiers from various families, and they were not treated harshly. They were wrapped tightly from head to toe to withstand the biting cold wind.

The speed of 60 miles a day was slower than Hudson expected. The main reason was that the roads along the way were too bad, which slowed down the speed of the convoy.

At this time, the benefits of having extensive social connections were reflected. By sending someone to inform them in advance, the nobles along the way would prepare what they needed.

Hot water, hot meals, hot dishes... everything is available. Basically, you can eat and stay along the way.

Since they were all friends, money was not an option. However, the sensible Hudson had prepared many generous gifts early on. Basically, he gave gifts as he went, so that the host would not suffer any loss.

Unfortunately, in a hurry to get on the road, he had to decline the welcome banquets held by the nobles along the way, and missed the opportunity to further exchange feelings and expand his circle.

However, there is no need to rush for this kind of thing. If you are willing to make friends, you can always find opportunities.

The proper arrangements made the knights, who were originally a little nervous, completely put their minds at ease.

There are our own people everywhere, so there is no reason to be afraid of the remnants of the Skull and Bones Society.

Soft power is also strength. The connections he demonstrated along the way made the knights look at Hudson differently.

Facts have proved that the famous "Divine Bow Knight" is not only a good fighter and has many relatives, but also has friends everywhere.

The most direct impact was on the Knights of Coelho, Roman and Valov. The three good neighbors privately expressed to Hudson their willingness to attach themselves to them.

Although there were signs of this before, they were just a prototype and had not been fully realized. What they saw and heard along the way undoubtedly made them determined.

The other knights also showed signs of moving closer, but due to the geographical location, they were unable to make up their minds.

The weaker the strength, the more it needs to work together. Before taking on this transportation business, Hudson had not felt the need in this regard.

After all, he was the local bully, and the strength of his neighbors was also average. He was lucky enough not to bully others, and he didn't worry about the threat of external enemies at all.

However, as the transportation business developed, Hudson suddenly found that his nest was not so safe, otherwise he would not have been so shameless as to threaten Kate to come to the Baron to help watch the door.

This kind of thing can only happen once or twice, but it must not happen again and again. If the oppression lasts for a long time, there will be a day when it will backfire.

It is unrealistic to significantly increase the strength of the territory in a short period of time, so Hudson has no choice but to turn his attention to the outside world.

With more friends, the road will be easier to walk; with more allies, you will be stronger in fighting.

However, forming an alliance is something that requires comprehensive consideration. While enjoying the benefits brought by the alliance, one must also assume corresponding obligations.

This kind of thing cannot be rushed, and we must constantly screen to select the most suitable allies to form a group with.
