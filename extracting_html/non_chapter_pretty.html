Please subscribe, vote, and reward...

For this book, Haiyue consulted information from more than 40 countries, and the reading content is estimated to be hundreds of millions of words.

There is no other way. It is difficult to find partial history. Often it takes several hours to look up the information for just two sentences in the book.

Regardless of accuracy, different data records often have different views on historical events.
