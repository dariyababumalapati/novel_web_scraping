Chapter 1035: Diverting trouble to the east

Stubbornly, it doesn’t exist. You must know that there is support from the British Empire behind them. Looking at the British warships in the Strait of Gibraltar opposite gives the chiefs unparalleled confidence.

The decadent Moroccan Sudanese government is worried, and the Spanish government, which is in endless troubles, is also not feeling well at the moment.

“Don’t have high expectations. The Kingdom of Morocco only has nominal rights to rule the tribes below. Even if it reaches an agreement with them, it cannot restrain the indigenous people below.

Spain’s face has been trampled to the ground. If we continue to let it go, everyone must know what will happen in the future. "

Left to the shoulders of the inefficient Spanish government, let alone that. We have meetings and discussions every day, but we can't come up with a feasible plan.

London
