Facts have proved that Ipamilonda's judgment was not wrong, and Crotocatacus's waiting was also correct. On the second day after Ipamilonda left Messenia, The Arcadian envoys arrived, headed by none other than Lycomedes.

Originally he just came to ask for help, but in Messenia he saw the powerful army of Theonia, and the Theonia army was fully helping the Messenians rebuild the new Messenian government and the city of Messenia .

After thinking about it, Lycomedes changed his mind and decided to form an alliance with Theonia.

Crotokatax held talks with him as the commander of the Theonian expeditionary force and as a prince.

After two days of consultation, the two sides initially drafted a resolution, the main content of which is: Theonia recognizes the Arcadian Alliance and ensures that the interests of its alliance are not infringed; the

Arcadian Alliance also recognizes Messenia , will maintain friendly exchanges with it;
Theonia will assist the Arcadian Alliance in building the city of Megalobilis as a symbol of friendship between the two parties;

Theonia and the Arcadian Alliance will form a military alliance, on one side When invaded by a powerful enemy, the other side will provide full assistance. And if we want to attack the enemy together, we will only target Sparta...

Judging from this alliance treaty, the Theonia side undoubtedly suffered some losses, but Crotokatax, Printols and others had reached an agreement during private discussions: as long as they can get the With the support of the people in the Arcadia region in the center of the Peloponnese (even if it is just neutral), Theonia's attack on Sparta will undoubtedly be much smoother, and it doesn't hurt to suffer some losses temporarily.

After the agreement was reached, Lycomedes did not stay in Messenia longer, and immediately returned to Arcadia, preparing to persuade the alliance so that the agreement could be finally passed.
