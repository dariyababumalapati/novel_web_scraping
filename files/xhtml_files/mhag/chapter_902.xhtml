For hundreds of years, the two most famous pirate groups on the Italian peninsula were the Illyrians on the Adriatic on the east coast and the Etruscans on the west coast. Unlike the Illyrians who were pirates mainly for survival, the Etruscan pirates were originally formed privately by the Etruscan city-states in order to combat the ongoing colonization of the Italian peninsula and began to threaten the Etruscans. The Greeks were hegemonic in central and northern Italy. As the wave of Greek colonization to the western Mediterranean weakened and the final spheres of influence of both sides were determined, Etruscan pirates decreased significantly.

But 30 years ago, the number of Etruscan pirates increased again. The reason was not due to official organization, but because the Celts in northern Italy began to invade southward and seize the Etruscan lands. Especially more than ten years ago, the Celtic army swept through the entire Etruscan territory and even captured Rome. Many Etruscans fled to the sea and became pirates. Even after the Gauls retreated, there were still some Some Etruscans, having tasted the sweetness of getting something for nothing, wanted to continue raiding the west coast of Italy.

However, during this period, due to the establishment of the third fleet of Theonia, which strengthened its patrols on the coast of the Kingdom of Theonia and its allies, the frequently attacked Etruscan pirates were greatly reduced again and could only huddle on the west coast of Italy. In the north, they dare not go south. Although Carthage once formed an alliance with Etruria and still maintains a tacit understanding of neighborly relations and non-aggression, Alenia has been invaded by Etruscan pirates in the past.

After Myontides' suggestion was adopted, Midorades began to make careful preparations. He even went to the newly occupied Etruscan city of Keslera to learn more about the Etruscan pirates. In order to cope with the situation, several Etruscan cargo ships and the armor and weapons of their soldiers were also borrowed.

Two days later, dozens of warships of the 3rd Fleet, loaded with fleet soldiers, quietly docked at nightfall on the beach between Alenia and the Etruscan town of Mariana in the north. 4,000 fleet soldiers The infantry landed ashore and sneaked into the mountains.

On the second day, several Etruscan ships suddenly broke into the offshore of Alenia and docked on the northern beach of Alenia. More than 200 "Etruscan pirates" landed ashore and rushed towards A famous silver mine in the mountains of northwest Alenia.

Ever since the Theonian army landed in the Latium area, the Alenians have been on tenterhooks, but a month or two later, the Theonian army did not land on Corsica, and even in the offshore area With no trace of the Theonian warships in sight, the Alenians were temporarily relieved. They thought that the Theonian army was still trapped in the war with Rome and could not get out. Unexpectedly, at this time, the hateful Itte Lurian pirates appeared, and they wanted to plunder a large silver mine in Alenia, which was an important source of revenue for the Alenia treasury.

After detailed exploration by the clipper, no other abnormalities were found offshore. Considering the difficulty of the Etruscan pirates, for the sake of safety, the Carthaginian judges in Alenia decided to deal with them as soon as possible to avoid Other accidents occurred, so he urged the city hall to quickly summon 800 Alenian citizen soldiers. He led this army out of the city and quickly rushed to the location of the silver mines.

When they launched an attack on the Etruscan pirates who were plundering the silver mines, 4,000 fleet infantry suddenly appeared behind them and surrounded them.

The Alenian army was quickly defeated, the Carthaginian judges died in the battle, and the soldiers were either killed or captured.

The fleet infantry first separated them into separate groups for interrogation, and then reunited and escorted them toward the beach. On the way, the Alesian prisoners at the front of the team suddenly rioted and knocked down the soldiers escorting them. Then the prisoners, whose faces were covered with blood and filth, returned to save their other companions.

Suddenly, the entire escort team fell into chaos. Many Alesians seized the opportunity to escape. By the time the fleet infantry calmed down the commotion, more than 200 prisoners had already fled towards the city of Alenia.

So, the fleet soldiers began to chase.

The prisoners ran away regardless of fatigue and fled to the city, shouting hysterically: "Open the door quickly!! The Theonians are coming! Open the door quickly!!..."

For hundreds of years, the Alenians have lived a peaceful and peaceful life under the protection of Carthage, except for occasionally fighting pirates. Suddenly they heard the news that "the Theonians were coming to kill", and the defenders at the top of the city They were all at a loss when they saw the disheveled and embarrassed compatriots under the city. The people shouting were all familiar people. The dust and smoke were billowing in the distance, and it was obvious that the enemy was approaching quickly.

So they opened the city gate without hesitation and urged the deserters to come in quickly.

As soon as the prisoners ran into the city, dozens of fleet infantry hidden among them pulled out the short swords tied to their backs and hidden by loose linings, and stabbed the prisoners beside them.

The city gate suddenly fell into chaos, causing a blockage and unable to be closed immediately.

The large fleet infantry force arrived soon after and took advantage of the situation to rush into the city...

After a period of fighting within the city, Alenia was captured.
