Not only was a small fortress built in just three months, but a new military camp was also built not far north of Leotini.

Paragonia Fortress is not a Lahe Fortress. It is only used to set up cards and charge fees. It is a real defensive fortress.

A 5-meter-wide and 5-meter-deep trench surrounds Paragoria. The bottom of the trench is covered with pointed wooden stakes, and both sides of the trench are filled with densely packed deer villages. This makes it very difficult for siege equipment to reach the city. difficulty.

Although the outer city wall is only 4 meters high, its structure is made of rammed earth, stone bricks, strips of stone and cement from the inside to the outside. It is not only thick and solid, but also smooth and flat with no obvious gaps. The city wall is also covered with thick lime Pink, this is not for aesthetics, but has a certain tactical effect. The white city walls will reflect light under the sun, which will affect the attacking soldiers, and will make it more difficult for ants to climb the city. At the same time, it can also reduce the stone The impact of the bullet on the city wall.

The circular surrounding outer city wall allows the defenders to better concentrate their forces for defense, and also allows reinforcements dispatched from the city to reach the city head more conveniently.

Every other section of the city wall has circular wall towers that protrude from the wall. They can strengthen the side attack on enemies huddled under the city or enemies attacking the city. At the same time, their circular walls can also weaken the impact of external forces. .

The walkway at the top of the city is not narrow, and the tops of the connected wall towers are even more spacious. A ballista and a belly bow are placed side by side on the top of each wall tower.
