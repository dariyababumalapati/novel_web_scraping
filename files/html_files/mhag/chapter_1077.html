Chapter 1077 Turiyi Household Registration Office
Maximus said, sighing in his heart: Even a small traffic problem has been so thoughtfully considered. He and his family quickened their pace, walked a few blocks, and asked a few passers-by before finally finding the household registration office.

The household registration office is located on the same street as the inspection office, tax office, construction fire station, urban court and other urban public departments. However, compared with other department buildings, the household registration office is obviously much larger in area, because people come to the household registration office to do business. There are obviously more people than other departments.

Today, the hall in the house is probably filled with people, and there are still many people crowding outside the door.

So, Marcim asked his wife and children to wait beside him, and hurried over alone.

When he got closer and heard people's heated discussions, Maximus realized that everyone was gathered here because yesterday all the household registration offices in Turiyi City posted notices: Temporarily suspending applications for Dionis for freedmen. We are waiting for notification when the processing of Asian citizenship will be resumed.

Maximus came in a hurry, but was suddenly poured a bottle of cold water on him, feeling depressed and uncomfortable.

I think the same was true for other free people. They lingered at the door, refusing to leave, talking to each other, venting their dissatisfaction, and hoping for a chance to see if there would be a change.

But no one dared to make a fuss or even cause a riot, not only because there was a row of heavily armed patrols standing at the door, but also because they were afraid that they would leave a criminal record that would affect their ability to become a formal citizen in the future.

Maximus sat among the crowd and listened as they speculated on the reasons for the suspension.

You must know that this is not the first time. Before the Western Mediterranean War, the Kingdom of Theonia also implemented this measure because there was not much land left, so a few people speculated whether this was the same reason this time.

But more people refute this, because after the Western Mediterranean War, the Kingdom of Dionysia acquired a large amount of land, and there is still a lot of land that is idle and is in urgent need of development.

Maximus's mood gradually calmed down. He looked back at his wife and children who were waiting eagerly on the street corner: Since he couldn't get the application for the time being, he'd better find a place to settle down first.

"Rent a house,” Maximus answered.

Maximus has already made it clear to the employees in the slave camp: if you want to rent a house, you don't have to go to Turiyi City to ask door to door, because most of the rental information is concentrated in the household registration offices in various urban areas.

Maximus walked into the household registration office and saw a spacious hall in front of him. Many people were already queuing up at various counters to handle matters.

Of course, the household registration office not only serves free people to become citizens, it also has many other tasks, such as: registering the criminal records of people in the kingdom, which is mainly provided by the nearby patrol offices and courts every day;

Registering the meritorious service information of the people of the Kingdom, large-scale meritorious service awards such as wars, large-scale public projects... If it is fake If found out, the punishment will be severe;
There is also the need to register the household registration changes of people in the Kingdom. People in the Kingdom who move from one town to another, or from one urban area to another urban area, need to register at the new urban household registration office. All the original household registration information about themselves will then be Everyone will be transferred, but the procedure is quite complicated and takes a long time, and it is easy to lose some materials and cause losses. Therefore, under normal circumstances, Kingdom residents will not change their household registration easily. Of course, freedmen who have not applied for citizenship are excepted. They are very mobile, but they must register when they move to a place. This is for the convenience of management. Otherwise, if they are found to be unregistered by the inspection station, they are likely to be deported...

Anyone who lives in the kingdom has the most contact with the public sector is the household registration office, and the affairs of the household registration office are also the most arduous among all departments. Small towns are better, but huge cities like Thurii with a population of hundreds of thousands , the personnel in each household registration office are busy every day, so the number of personnel under their jurisdiction is also the largest among the kingdom's departments.

The reason why each household registration office occupies a large area is that more than half of its space is used to store residents' information.

The unique household registration system of the Kingdom of Dioria has caused two important problems: First, it requires a large number of people who can write records.
