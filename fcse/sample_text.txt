  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     On the way back, Hero was restless. He knew from the tone of Demosthenes' last words that the general was not interested in his suggestions. While scolding Demosthenes for being stubborn, he once again strengthened his determination to carry out his original plan.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     In fact, at that time, most Greek generals were stubborn and insisted on their own views. Before the Battle of Salamis, after failing to convince the leaders of the Greek coalition forces to accept the proposal of a decisive battle with the Persians at sea for several days, the Athenian general Themistocles had to use a strategy to let the Persian navy blockade Salamis to force the Greeks to fight. In the subsequent Battle of Plataea, the Greek coalition forces confronted the Persian army in Plataea. The Greek coalition forces decided to retreat to a relatively advantageous position, but a Spartan general resolutely refused to retreat and spent the whole night in quarrels. On the second day, the rest of the troops retreated, but the central force of Sparta remained in place. As a result, it was surrounded by the Persian cavalry who came after hearing the news. The Greek coalition forces had to fight a decisive battle with the enemy. If the Persian general had not accidentally fallen off his horse and died, the outcome of the Greco-Persian War might have been rewritten.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     The stubborn nature of Greek generals comes from their culture. The Greeks who grew up in the Greek city-state system and culture advocate freedom, love thinking, like independence, pursue personal honor, and are extremely confident individuals who will not be easily changed by the will of others.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     This incident taught Shiloh a small lesson. He walked forward while thinking, and before he knew it, he had arrived at his own military tent.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Timasus, who was waiting there anxiously, came forward and asked: "Helo, what is the general calling you for?"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Hilo looked at him with a heavy face: "The general is planning to launch a night raid on Syracuse in the next two days, and he asked me to participate."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Oh!" Timasus opened his mouth wide and cried out in amazement: "This is true! It...it came true!"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Shiloh made a gesture to silence her: "This is a military secret, you can't tell anyone."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Timasus hesitated to speak and looked at Hero eagerly.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Hiro was very satisfied with Timasus's reaction, but his face remained calm: "This is only half of the truth. We need to wait and see."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "You...you should remind the general." Timasus seemed a little unwilling.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Brother, who do you think I am! Of course I reminded him. Even though the gods warned me to keep it secret, I still gave him suggestions in a tactful way out of my love for Athens. But not only did he not listen, he also drove me out of his tent. If I had told him the prophecy directly, I guess he would have killed me as a spy." Hero vented his anger.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Then...what should I do?" Timasus muttered to himself in confusion.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Hiro looked at him with contempt. The more he interacted with Timasus, the more he realized that this young man was too immature. However, on the other hand, it was a good thing for Hiro.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "I was wondering who has such a loud voice. It turns out it's Shiloh who's back. He made me wait so long!" A man walked out of the military tent with a smile.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Shiloh, I forgot to tell you that Uncle Nur brought a bucket of sea fish to see you and waited for nearly an hour①."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Uncle Nur? Nurnusis! Shiloh's eyes lit up, he was really looking forward to Cao Cao, Cao Cao coming! In Shiloh's memory, he admired Nurnusis very much. That was because when he was a teenager, he had taken Nurnusis's cargo ship several times and witnessed his unique courage as a seaman fighting against the wind and waves.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Shiloh came forward with a smile: "If I had known that the uncle was coming, I should have come back later."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis was stunned for a moment, then laughed: "The little bull's injury was not in vain, he has learned to joke."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Hilo endured the strong fishy smell from Nurnusis and gave him a strong hug. "The harbor is so unsafe right now, but you still went out on a boat to fish for me. I really appreciate it!"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "It's not easy to catch these cod. Our boat was almost caught by the patrol boat of Syracuse. I deserve your thanks," said Nurnusis without any modesty.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Then let's not talk about these empty words. When we return to Athens, I will ask Uncle Solmus to let you take on more work."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis sent the fish because he wanted to build a relationship with the young master of the Shiloh family. Although the Shiloh family's business was now managed by Sulmus, Shiloh was already an adult and could take over his family's business at any time. He made some emotional investment now, which would be useful in the future. Unexpectedly, Shiloh saw through his intentions at a glance and immediately promised him benefits.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     How come this child has become smarter after not seeing him for a few days?
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis was in a daze, and Shiloh continued, "Uncle, is the beach management very lax? Can you go into the sea at will?"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "We are just fulfilling our civic duty. We sent you here and will send you back to Athens. The council will pay us. We will also see if we can get some spoils here. We are not the navy and are not responsible for fighting. Although we have to follow the arrangements of the navy, we are free."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Hilo was delighted when he heard this and asked again, "Does Syracuse blockade this great port tightly?"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis looked at Shiloh in surprise: "Hey, when did you start to care about these things? Do you want to run away?" As he said that, he laughed again.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Shiloh didn't laugh. At this moment, countless thoughts were running through his mind. Finally, he decided to take a gamble: "If I really want to escape, will the uncle help me?"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     One stone can cause a thousand ripples!
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis and Timasu beside him were stunned.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Hiro, could it be that... could it be that you have received the oracle? This expedition will..." Timasus just opened his mouth in panic when he was interrupted by Hiro: "I didn't receive it. Didn't you hear it clearly? This is just a hypothesis."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Timasus breathed a sigh of relief, but then he thought: What does Hero mean by suddenly saying this?
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis was confused and couldn't help but interrupt: "What are you two talking about?"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Uncle, Hero received an oracle that we will launch a night attack on Syracuse, and in the end we... we will be defeated miserably. Now this prophecy has been half fulfilled." There was no need for Hero to explain, Timasus could not help but answer first.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Hero glanced at him seriously, and Timasus immediately realized that he had leaked the secret. Little did he know that this was exactly what Hero expected.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Oracle? Nurnus looked at Shiloh. In the dark night, Shiloh's eyes were bright and deep, making it hard to look at them. The rumor that Shiloh was saved by the gods had made Nurnus half-believe it because it was spread by word of mouth. Now he was 70% convinced. Shiloh was completely different in appearance and temperament. Was this really the blessing of the gods? !
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Aren't oracles only available to priests?" Nurnusis, of course, saw their little actions and became even more curious, so he deliberately provoked them.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Xi Luo coughed dryly and said helplessly: "Uncle, you are not an outsider, and you take care of us so much. Logically, I shouldn't hide anything from you, but the general repeatedly warned me to keep it strictly confidential, so I-"
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusis looked at Timasus and thought: It was said to be a secret, how did this guy know it?
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     He was angry, so he spoke stiffly: "You are being suspicious, kid. I don't want to hear any secrets from you. It's getting late. I should go back."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Uncle, don't be angry. I told you." Xi Luo hurriedly grabbed him and said, "But, don't tell anyone else after you listen to it."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "I've been sailing for so many years and have encountered a lot of things. I can tell the difference between the important and the unimportant," said Nurnusis unhappily.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Shiloh told Nurnusis the "pictures" he saw during his illness.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     After listening to this, Nurnusis was silent for a long time, and then slowly said: "Whether this is true or not will be revealed in the next two days."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "I really hope these images are just my hallucinations." Shiloh sighed.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "We shouldn't just wait, we should go and dissuade the general!" Timasus said excitedly.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     Nurnusius looked at Hero and then at Timathus, and thought: Hero used to be reckless and Timathus was quiet, but now the situation is reversed.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "Demosthenes is an extremely conceited guy. You'd better not provoke him." Nurnusis warned solemnly.
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     "No matter what happens, you two must protect yourselves first." Nurnusis stared at Shiloh until he nodded: "Sulmus told me to take good care of you two. If you have any difficulties, you can come to the dock to find me."
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
   <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
    <font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
     This sentence is exactly what Shiloh needs!
    </font>
   </font>
  </p>
  <p>
