Chapter 205: True and False Inheritance Holy Land

As soon as the bank's affairs were dealt with, Hudson's attention was drawn to the orcs. The location of the inheritance holy land could not be determined, but the location of the orc tribe was easy to find.

A group of masters of the kingdom have secretly entered the Snow Moon Territory, waiting for a perfect opportunity to make a move.

If you fall behind, you will be beaten. The Orc Empire, which lacks the support of Sanctuary masters, is already at a strategic disadvantage.

Relying on the orcs' background, it would be no problem to set up traps in advance and kill one or two powerful Sanctuary warriors, but facing four, five, six, seven or eight powerful Sanctuary warriors, there would be absolutely no point.

It so happened that the Alpha Kingdom now had eight powerful saints. Their own people were definitely not enough, so they would just need to add those from their allies.

The four allies each sent a powerful Sanctuary to help. Together with the two Sanctuaries from the kingdom and Hudson's dragon-bear combination, there were a total of eight Sanctuaries.

If the orcs' holy land of heritage were exposed, it would immediately be struck by thunder from eight top warriors.

If Hudson had not been afraid that his actions would attract interference from the Sanctuary of the alien alliance, he would have already taken a group of Sanctuary warriors to the Orc Empire to cause damage.

There are knights and mages. This combination is not just "1+...+1=8". When they cooperate with each other, they can often burst out more powerful combat power.

Even if you encounter several main orc legions head-on, you can still break out of the siege directly.

If they abandon their moral principles and do not engage in direct combat but instead launch a sneak attack, the scene will be even more tragic.

In the past, Hudson didn't dare to go out and show off, because other people's traps might not be able to kill dragons and bears, but his master was not strong enough to withstand a beating!

If Maxim and Bear Stearns each have a combat power of 100, with him as their master, it would be considered good to have 120.

If you hold your teammates back, you must be aware of the situation. You must not do anything that will lead to your own death.

This time the situation was different, and they had enough strength to face the danger. The only problem was that they had to be fast, and they had to finish it before the alien alliance intervened.

Who made it so that the orcs not only had no powerful people in the holy realm, but even their inherited artifacts fell into the hands of humans?

In the capital, Hudson had seen the orcs' inherited artifacts: "Shaman Horn", "Sacrificial Song"...

However, these artifacts are incomplete. The Alpha Kingdom only obtained a part of them, and the rest are in the hands of other major human forces.

This was not just for the orcs, all other races were treated the same. As long as the racial artifacts fell into the hands of the human race, they would be dismantled and destroyed, and then kept by the major forces.

Some parts were treasured, some were sealed in secret places, and some were directly exiled to another dimension.

Even if an alien race is lucky enough to find a part, it is difficult to piece together a complete artifact. It can be said that once these racial artifacts fall into the hands of humans, they will basically be useless.

Of course, there are exceptions. As long as the artifact is not so picky about the owner and the human race can use it normally, they can escape.

Without the inherited artifact, many methods could not be used. Otherwise, the eighth-level strongman could use the artifact to fight against the Holy Land.

If it is in the hands of a strong person in the Holy Land, its power will be even greater.

"Everyone, from the intelligence we have gathered so far, there are three possible locations where the orc heritage holy land will be born this time, in the areas controlled by the bear people, lion people, and elephant people.

There is no doubt that at least two things here are false.

Unfortunately, the orcs were deliberately divided into three waves, and now we have no way to verify it further."

Hudson said helplessly.

He originally thought that by tracking the movements of talented young people from various races, he would be able to find the location of the place of inheritance, but he never expected that the enemy would do this to him.

Originally, only one hundred people from each race were needed to participate, but this time each of the major orc races selected three hundred people, and some of the smaller races almost sent out their entire tribe.

The intelligence department has worked very hard, but except for a few high-ranking orcs who know the truth, the many races below are all confused.

"Your Excellency Marshal, I heard that to participate in the Orc Heritage Trial, one must be a genius. Can you work hard on this?"

After hearing Archbishop Terran's question, Hudson shook his head and replied, "Archbishop, there is no specific standard for genius.

Some small races are just short of having the entire clan participate. It's impossible that all of their clan members are geniuses, right?

Combined with the analysis of intelligence collected from the Orc Empire, I think it is a fallacy that those who participate in the inheritance trial must be geniuses.

The truth is that all orcs can participate, but geniuses have a higher chance of obtaining the inheritance. Each tribe sends talented children to participate, in fact, they are competing for a higher success rate.

After all, the Beast God also has his status. If anyone can inherit it, it would be too cheap.

According to previous statistics, as long as the orcs who received the inheritance did not die young, they could at best become eighth-rank warriors, and the probability of breaking through the holy realm was also very high. "

Hudson's reinterpretation of the orc heritage trial left everyone stunned.

If this inference is correct, then all their previous inherent understandings about the matter would be overturned.

Although they were unwilling to accept it in their hearts, their reason told them that this explanation was the most reliable.

There has never been a specific standard for "genius". Just by looking at performance in youth, no one can be sure which member of the tribe is the most talented.

No matter how you screen, no matter what criteria you use, there is no way to guarantee that the one hundred geniuses you choose are the most talented in the clan.

Not to mention the holy land of inheritance left by the Beast God, even if he came here in person, he would probably not be able to select the best seedlings from so many orcs.

"If that's the case, things are going to be troublesome!

Marshal, I wonder what plans you have for the next move?"

Archbishop Terran asked solemnly.

A sneak attack can also be regarded as a military operation. Since it is a military operation, we naturally have to listen to the opinions of professionals.

"There is no other way, we can only improvise and try our luck.

We'll see how the enemy reacts. No matter how much we fake it, it won't be exactly the same. We'll attack the side with more high-ranking officials and more main forces in the Orc Empire.

Even if we cannot destroy the enemy's heritage site, killing some of the orc high-ranking officials will not be in vain.

Another plan is to directly capture the elites of each race regardless of whether the orcs' heritage land has been revealed.

As long as we are fast enough and take care of one of the routes first, it will definitely cause panic among the orcs.

Then just look for opportunities in the chaos!"

Hudson said expressionlessly.

Originally, he wanted to wipe out some of the smaller races so that the orcs would not have enough races to open the holy land. Hudson even sent people to find out the locations of the various races.

Unexpectedly, the enemy was well prepared. Before the kingdom could take action, the selected talented boys and girls dispersed and left the tribe.

It is really difficult to find a team on the vast grassland.

What's more, it is in the enemy's territory and there is no way to track it.

When it comes to professional issues, Archbishop Tyron is completely confused. Robert next to him knows a little, but that's just a little, and he can't give reliable advice.

The other four saints came to help. They were not familiar with the situation in the Orc Empire at all and were distracted from the beginning.

No one had any opinions, so the decision-making power fell to Hudson. As this matter was of great importance, he did not dare to make a decision on his own.

After all, the information was collected too smoothly. We are not afraid that two of the three locations are false, but we are afraid that all three locations are false.

If the direction is chosen wrong, no matter how perfect the plan is, it will not play its due role.

After all, once the tacit understanding is broken, the strong men in the Holy Land will put aside their pride and directly carry out assassination, which no one can stand!

Although this kind of illegal move is likely to happen only once, even if it happens once, it is still fatal.

Lure out the enemy is secondary. The key is to "kill the chicken to scare the monkey" and let the human race know that the Orc Empire is not their back garden and they can come whenever they want.

Apart from Hudson who is out in the open, there has been no news of the birth of a powerful Sanctuary in the Alpha Kingdom so far. This is obviously abnormal!
