It turns out that problems cannot be solved through discussion alone.

After taking stock of all the countries on the continent, Pius VII simply couldn't find a single ally of any importance.

Except for the Holy See's little brothers, the positions of various countries can generally be divided into two types: directly anti-Holy See and neutrally anti-Holy See.

The former is represented by the Frankish Kingdom and the Alpha Kingdom, while the latter is represented by the Inlibyan Kingdom, the three kingdoms of the Central Continent, and many small countries on the continent.

Of course, the actual actions of the parties are one thing and their verbal performance is another.

When the news of the establishment of a new church in the Frankish Kingdom spread, the Libyans were the first to condemn it, and then their church also started to make small moves.

The performance of the three countries in the Central Continent was also very interesting. On the one hand, they called on all parties to remain calm, and on the other hand, they sent people to participate in the separate papal conference initiated by the Frankish Kingdom.

The Alpha Kingdom only condemned the atrocities of the Holy See and did not come out to make its presence felt again, but their church has completely copied all of the Holy See's institutions.

The intention of rebellion is out in the open, so no one would be surprised if a Northern Continent Church emerges one day.

Fortunately, the Human Alliance Parliament is in recess, otherwise if the commotion continues like this, the Alliance will be destroyed.

Mediation is impossible. Several major forces of the human race are the protagonists in the turmoil. Who of the remaining small countries dares to make any comments?

No one within the human race wanted to stop the fight, and the alien alliance outside was eager for them to fight directly, and everyone was busy fanning the flames from behind.

Fortunately, the gods are caring for the human race. The internal conflicts among the major human forces and the internal contradictions of the alien alliance are gradually intensified.

It is not easy to share adversity, and it is even harder to share prosperity.

The previous distribution of spoils had already caused conflicts between several ethnic groups in the Central Continent, and the current imbalance in internal trade had further exacerbated such conflicts.

The protagonist of the turmoil is the Dwarf Kingdom, which is at the top of the trade chain by virtue of its monopoly in the arms market.

The elves are also vested interests. The dwarves forge weapons and equipment, and they produce magicians to carve magic circles for the equipment, occupying an advantageous position in the field of high-end arms.

A piece of ordinary equipment could be exchanged for tons of food and ore. Magical equipment was even more priceless, often requiring a tribe of 10,000 people to work for several years.

At the beginning, no one noticed any problem, after all, this was the market price.

The high prices of weapons are due not only to technological monopoly but also to information opacity.

In the eyes of all ethnic groups, these advanced weapons must be very expensive, so it is normal for them to be so expensive.

But as time went by, the situation gradually changed.

Everyone was surprised to find that the production cost of these weapons and equipment was much lower than they had expected.

Three to five times the profit is just a discount. Ten times the profit is the norm.

It is possible that some masterpieces could be priced a hundred times higher than the production cost.

Knowing more inevitably made people unbalanced. The various tribes, unwilling to be exploited, jointly asked the dwarves to lower their prices.

There is no doubt that such "unreasonable demands" will naturally not be agreed to.

The weapons of the dwarves have always been in short supply, and we only supply them because they are allies.

If you want to buy it outside, you have to pay extra before you can get the goods.

If you want to buy, buy it. If you don’t want to buy, forget it. When dwarves do business, they are straightforward.

The orcs suffered the most. Because of the distance, they had to go through a long detour through the territory of the alien alliance to purchase weapons and equipment.

After paying the tolls along the way, the prices, which were already in the sky, floated directly out of the atmosphere.

The high price is not the worst thing. What is even more tragic is that they are pure buyers and domestic products cannot be sold at all.

It’s not that the allies are deliberately lowering the price, it’s entirely due to the geographical location.

The sea trade routes are blocked by the Alpha Kingdom, and the land transportation lines must first cross the ice and snow world before they can trade with the ancient remnant alliance.

Poor transportation conditions have caused most of the goods they produce to lose their trading value.

The only things that can be traded are some hard currencies, such as magic crystals, magic cores, and magic materials.

Against this background, the Orc Empire did not purchase many weapons and equipment, but instead saw a large outflow of wealth.

In the past, the best way to quell a trade deficit was to take the army to rob the human race.

At this moment, the various tribes within the Orc Empire were busy fighting among themselves and were simply unable to work together, let alone looting.

Of course, the most important reason is that there is nothing to steal now.

The Near East is only the Alpha Kingdom's territory on paper. The port is still being selected and there is no sign of anyone there.

The seven provinces in the northern border had all just been ravaged by disasters, and only the land and the remaining castles were left. The areas further north were poor and hard to come by.

Perhaps in the hearts of many orc tribes, they are still looking forward to the Alpha Kingdom developing the Near East as soon as possible and cultivating the "leeks".

Everyone had a lot of trouble to deal with, and the continent of Aslant became peaceful. Except for the occasional fights by the orc tribes, the overall situation gradually stabilized.

The controversy over the establishment of a new church is still going on, but Hudson is no longer worried. If the church does not take action immediately, it means that diplomatic means will be used to resolve the issue.

Obviously, this was a forced result.

Without the support of other countries, the Holy See had absolutely no chance of winning the long expedition to the Frankish Kingdom.

If you want to 'kill the chicken to scare the monkey', you can't find a suitable target.

Those who are directly against the Holy See are not easy to deal with. There are so many heretics on the mainland. If you suffer heavy losses by accident, you will be really doomed.

Those who are neutral and anti-Vatican are slightly easier to bully, but the political impact is too bad.

It is possible that the "chicken" was not killed, but instead the neutral factions were forced into a direct anti-Vatican camp, making the situation even worse.

The vested interests are not stupid, and they will naturally weigh the pros and cons. The more issues they consider, the less they dare to act.

Seeing the verbal battle between the two sides, Hudson was relieved. As long as there was no fighting in the short term, no one expected the two hegemons to live in peace in the future.

If the Frankish Kingdom and the Holy See really reconcile, the Alpha Kingdom will be doomed.

In the past, the kingdom dared to go against the Holy See, not only because the two sides were far apart, but most importantly because it had the Franks in front to support it.

In recent years, whenever the Holy See took major action, the Frankish Kingdom would inevitably lag behind, and vice versa.

Only when the two major powers hinder each other can other countries live a good life.

After putting down the intelligence in his hand, Hudson continued to improve the development plan of Xueyue Territory with his staff.

“Duke, according to the kingdom’s Near East strategic plan, we will be the first batch of pioneers.

After considering various factors, the Government Affairs Department believes that the initial development of the territory can focus on the port.

The orcs lack navigation technology, so even if something unexpected happens, we can evacuate by sea.

The first batch of immigrants were mainly fishermen, which could not only solve the meat problem of the front-line garrisons, but also avoid the threat of orcs.

The Ministry of Government Affairs plans to send 3,000 fishermen to Crescent Port after the port is successfully opened.

After gaining a firm foothold, the number of immigrants will be gradually increased.

Judging from the progress of the port development, immigration work will begin next year. It is estimated that in the 99994th year of the Holy Calendar, we will migrate 20,000 people to the Snow Moon Territory.

Three years later, the total number of immigrants in the area will exceed 100,000!"

Jacob said with great enthusiasm.

They are all beneficiaries of the eastward development of Snow Moon Territory. Although Hudson's pie-in-the-sky technology is very rudimentary, it is definitely a breakthrough in the Aslant Continent.

In order to motivate his men to work hard, Master Hudson has already painted a beautiful picture for everyone after the development of Xueyue Territory is completed.

Status, wealth, and splendor were all there. In order to guard against orcs, Hudson even created a unique new aristocracy system.

Of course there are fiefdoms and titles, but everyone does not need to manage them individually. Instead, he, the feudal lord, will be responsible for unified planning and management, and everyone can directly receive dividends from the profits.

Under normal circumstances, doing this would definitely be considered as a malicious act, but the Near East is an exception.

With the orcs as good neighbors, it is a miracle for the territories of small and medium-sized nobles to balance income and expenditure. If they are not careful, they will die and their territories will be destroyed.

Rather than living in crisis every day, it is more reliable to unify management and share risks.

The power is restricted, but the advantage is that security is improved and the initial development investment is saved!

For new nobles, the territory reclamation period is the most prone to accidents. Many noble lords have fallen at this most critical stage.

It was easy to follow Master Hudson. According to the plan for building a new aristocratic system, they were directly exempted from the initial risks.

As for subsequent rights issues, first join the potential group of nobility and consider it after accumulating the first pot of gold in your life!

The only drawback is that the threshold for joining this plan is very high. Only retainers who have made great contributions are eligible, which is more difficult than obtaining a title.

"There is nothing wrong with the plan, but it is too conservative. If we follow your plan, the Kingdom's Near East strategy will not be completed on time.

Crescent Harbor is not only the port of Snow Moon Territory, but also the largest military port of the Kingdom in the Near East. In addition to undertaking the development of the territory, it also supports the construction of the territories of other nobles.

简单的来说，新月港开辟过后，我们不光要在当地站稳脚跟，还要为一众近东领主提供后续的物质保障。

首批移民三千可以不变，但明年我们最少要向近东输送五万移民，三年后当地的总人口不能低于三十万。

考虑到兽人的反应，前期的移民聚居点，全部放在沿海地区好了。

把思维模式打开，雪月领的疆域范围，只有和人族接壤区域有边界，其它区域都是没有边界的。

我记得兽人帝国的东部沿海多是沼泽地、盐碱地，当地很少有兽人部落聚居，这就是机会。

不适合农牧，又不影响捕鱼，未来很长一段时间内，雪月领的支柱产业都是渔业。

寻找适合防守的地区，先设立聚居点。

那边的地形不适合大兵团展开，兽人就算是过来袭击，兵力也不会太多。

我们坐拥海上交通的便利，很容易在局部战场对敌人形成兵力优势。

前期的移民一律不带家卷，平时为民，战时为兵。

我会和王国的魔法师团打招呼，让他们顺便清理一下近东沿海的礁石，以确保三千吨以下的船舶能够自由通航！”

哈德逊的话，将众人吓了一大跳。

只能在内心深处感慨一句：不愧是王国的元帅，做事就是霸气!

拿魔法师团的法师老爷们当劳工用，众人是想都不敢想。

当然，这种事情也就哈德逊干得出来。别的贵族敢向魔法师团提出这要求，就等着挨揍吧！

近海暗礁被清理，海上大动脉畅通，一切都不一样了。

整体上，王国在近东地区依旧处于被动，但局部地区却获得了主动权。

要是沿着兽人帝国沿海一路清理下去，未来的兽人大草原，四处都是王国军队的登陆点。

当然，这只是理想状态。兽人一旦发现了王国的计划，必定会采取措施破坏。

“公爵，移民计划一下子扩大这么多，领地的运力怕是无法满足。

尤其是前期阶段，近东开发战略基础设施建设、王国的驻军、全部都要靠从海上获得补给。

估摸着东南沿海的闲置运力，都会被王国征用，就算是花钱也很难雇佣到足够的船舶。

主要还是幽灵船之祸的后遗症，外国船舶对这条航线心有余季，很少有船东接单。

如果想要计划顺利展开的话，还需要您协调一下和沿海几家大贵族的关系，尤其是和道尔顿家族的关系。”

凯恩顺势提议道。

指挥老大干活，这也没谁了。但这种活儿，也只有哈德逊能干。

其他人出面，连这些大贵族的人都见不到，更不用说让人家挤出宝贵的运力帮忙了。

“那就替我邀约皮尔斯伯爵，现在的局势不一样了，我们两家之间的关系也该发生变化。”

哈德逊笑呵呵的说道。

贵族的世界就是如此，敌友从来都不是绝对的。

以往的时候，科斯洛家族和道尔顿家族一起窝在东南行省，那就是妥妥的竞争对手。

现在科斯洛家族战略东移，继续和道尔顿家族在东南行省斗下去，就有些得不偿失了。

短时间内，确实是东南行省的土地更加肥沃，经济更加发达，但未来就不一定了。

雪月领最大的优势就是边界模湖，除了和一众王国领主接壤的地区有边界外，剩下的区域都没有明确划分。

理论上来说，只要哈德逊有能力，完全可以一直往兽人帝国扩张。

这种扩张是有法理支撑的。无论是人族联盟，还是阿尔法王国都法律明文规定：鼓励人族领主向异族扩张。

用一句话概括，那就是所有从异族手中抢来的土地，那都是合法的！

不管异族是否承认，反正人类世界是承认的。

理论上来说，一介平民从异族手中抢到一片土地，只要能够守的住，也可以合法的成为当地领主。

至于爵位的高低，从来都没人想过这个问题，因为这张大饼压根儿就没人吃到过。

当人族敢把这样的条文以法律形式写出来时，自身已经成了大陆霸主，贵族体系早就完善了。
